## FAQ

### Is this really free?
Yes, 100% free. No login, no credit card, no trial period. We make money by offering paid consultations to teams with high savings potential. The audit itself is and will always be free.

### How do you calculate savings?
We compare your current plan and seat count against official vendor pricing data. Our engine checks three things: (1) whether your plan tier matches your team size, (2) whether an alternative tool could deliver comparable value at a lower price for your use case, and (3) whether you might have unused seats. All pricing is sourced from official vendor pages and verified regularly.

### Do you share my data with AI vendors?
Never. Your audit data is stored privately and is never shared with any AI tool vendor. The shareable link you receive is optional and contains no personally identifiable information.

### How often is pricing data updated?
We verify pricing against official vendor pages at least monthly. The "Last verified" date is shown on every audit result. AI tool pricing changes frequently — we track it so you don't have to.

### What if the audit says I'm already optimized?
That's great news! It means you're on the right plans for your team size and use case. We'll still save your audit so you can check back when pricing changes or your team grows.

### Can I share my results with my team?
Yes. Every audit generates a unique shareable link that you can send to your budget holder, post in Slack, or share on social media. The link shows the full audit breakdown without any personal information.

### I manage a large team. Can you help with enterprise pricing?
If your audit shows more than $500/month in potential savings, we'll offer a free consultation to explore enterprise discount programs and volume licensing. This is completely optional.
